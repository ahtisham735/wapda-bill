Simplifying Your Electricity Management: A Guide to FESCO Online Services

Check Your FESCO Bill

1. How to Check FESCO Bill Online

Gone are the days of waiting in long queues or dealing with paper bills. With FESCO's online bill checking service , you can access your electricity bill anytime, anywhere. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official FESCO website.
  2. Look for the 'Online Bill' or 'Bill Inquiry' section.
  3. Enter your 14-digit reference number (printed on your bill).
  4. Click on 'Submit' or 'View Bill' to access your current bill details.
  5. You can also choose to download or print the bill for your records.

2. How to Pay FESCO Bill Online

Paying your electricity bill has never been easier thanks to FESCO's online payment options. Here's how you can settle your bill electronically:

  1. Visit FESCO's official website.
  2. Navigate to the 'Online Bill Payment' section.
  3. Enter your reference number or consumer ID.
  4. Choose your preferred payment method (credit/debit card, mobile wallet, or online banking).
  5. Follow the prompts to complete the payment securely.
  6. Once the transaction is successful, you'll receive a confirmation message.

3. How to Apply for a New FESCO Connection

Need a new electricity connection? FESCO simplifies the process with its online application system. Follow these steps to apply for a new connection:

  1. Visit the FESCO website and locate the New Connection section.
  2. Fill out the online application form with accurate details.
  3. Attach the required documents, such as CNIC, proof of ownership, and site plan.
  4. Submit the application and wait for FESCO's verification process.
  5. Once approved, pay the necessary fees online.
  6. FESCO will schedule the installation of your new connection.

4. FESCO History

5. FESCO Bill Taxes

Understanding the components of your electricity bill can help you manage your expenses better. FESCO bills typically include charges for electricity consumption, taxes, surcharges, and other levies imposed by the government. Be sure to review your bill carefully to understand the breakdown of charges.

6. Tips to Reduce FESCO Bill

Looking to lower your electricity bill? Here are some tips to help you reduce energy consumption and save money:

  • Use energy-efficient appliances and lighting.
  • Turn off lights and electronics when not in use.
  • Opt for natural ventilation instead of air conditioning.
  • Seal gaps and cracks to prevent energy loss.
  • Consider investing in solar panels or other renewable energy sources.

7. Safety Guidelines

When dealing with electricity, safety should always be a top priority. Here are some essential safety guidelines to keep in mind:

  • Avoid tampering with electrical equipment or meters.
  • Hire qualified professionals for electrical installations and repairs.
  • Keep electrical outlets and wires away from water sources.
  • Use surge protectors to safeguard sensitive electronics.
  • In case of emergencies or power outages, contact FESCO's helpline immediately.
